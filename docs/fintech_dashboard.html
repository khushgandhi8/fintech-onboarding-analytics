Recommendation & Next Steps
Do not roll out the $5 blanket bonus. While statistically significant, the unit economics are negative at current ARPU. Two paths forward exist: reduce the bonus or improve retention.
1
Test a $3 targeted bonus on paid-channel users only. This reduces cost while targeting the highest-intent segment. Paid users show ~23% baseline deposit rate — the bonus amplifies an already-strong cohort rather than subsidizing low-intent signups.
2
Investigate the bank-link drop-off (64.4%). This is the largest single leak in the funnel and is likely a UX problem, not an incentive problem. Fixing it has zero marginal cost and directly improves all downstream metrics.
3
Invest in post-deposit retention to raise ARPU toward $30+. If feature engagement or premium conversion can be improved, the economics of the $5 bonus flip positive — and no experiment changes are needed.
